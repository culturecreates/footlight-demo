Introducing Footlight

A simple tool that dramatically improves findability of your events

Welcome

Footlight helps you automatically manage your metadata.

We know your time is precious so we desiged Footlight to have absolutely no data entry. All metadata is crawled from public websites and public data sources and then translated by Footlight into machine "understandable" code. The end result is a dramatic increase in the find-ability of your events.

Please sign up to the Footlight beta waiting list.

Knowledge graph

We are just beginning, but you can already preview the aggregate knowledge graph. Footlight crawls all websites related to performing arts in Canada. The aggregate Linked Open Data (LOD) is used by virtual assistants like Alexa and data consumers like Expedia, AirBnb and Google. If your website is not being crawled then please sign-up to add it.
