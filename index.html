Introducing Footlight

A simple tool that dramatically improves findability of your events

Welcome

At Culture Creates we believe that every arts organization and artist is entitled to their own knowledge graph as a means of expressing themselves to machines on the Web. We hope this portal will assist you and your organization with your digital presense. Please sign up.

Knowledge graph

We are just beginning, but you can already preview the aggregate knowledge graph. Some techies call this Linked Open Data or LOD. The aggregate data can be used by virtual assistants like Alexa and data consumers like Expedia, AirBnb and Google to help people find your events. Footlight is a bot that crawls the web like Google and learns about events related to performing arts.
